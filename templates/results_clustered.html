Survival

You can also upload survival data to do a survival analysis on the calculated clusters. The file must meet the following requirements:

  • The first column is the sample id
  • The second column is the followup time (as a number).
  • The third column is the status at end of followup (0=no event, 1=event).
  • The time is a number with no unit. Decimals are ok, using a dot.
  • The fields are separated with tabs (\t).
  • All sample ids in the survival file must also exist in the clustering result.
